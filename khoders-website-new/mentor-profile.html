Professional Biography

Daniel Osei is an experienced web developer and passionate educator with over 5 years of professional development experience. As the Lead Web Development Mentor at KHODERS, he guides students through their coding journey from basic concepts to advanced full-stack applications.

With a background in both frontend and backend technologies, Daniel specializes in modern JavaScript frameworks, responsive design, and best practices for building scalable applications. His teaching philosophy emphasizes hands-on learning, practical problem-solving, and building real-world projects that students can showcase in their portfolios.
