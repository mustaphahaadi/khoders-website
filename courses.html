Admissions & Eligibility

Our programs welcome students and recent graduates who are excited to dedicate time each week to building their skills. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis with priority given to active campus members.

What we look for

  • Curiosity and commitment to collaborative learning
  • Availability for 8-10 hours per week of workshops and project work
  • Basic familiarity with computers and internet access
  • Completion of the KHODERS community pledge

How to apply

  1. Submit the program interest form with your goals and availability.
  2. Attend a 15-minute fit conversation with a mentor advisor.
  3. Complete a short challenge aligned with your track of choice.
  4. Receive cohort placement and onboarding instructions within 5 business days.

Need support? We reserve seats for first-time coders and offer scholarship assistance for students with financial need. Email info@khodersclub.com to learn more.

Program FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about joining KHODERS programs.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No. Our Foundations track is designed for beginners and will guide you through the basics before advancing to collaborative projects.

How much time should I commit each week?

Plan for 6 hours of structured sessions plus 2-4 hours of self-paced project work. Mentors will help you plan around your course load.

Can I switch tracks after enrolling?

Yes. During Weeks 1-2 you can move into a different track with advisor approval to ensure the new cohort has space.

Is there a cost to join?

Participation is free for KHODERS members. Optional certification exams or travel for events may have supplemental fees, which we announce in advance.

Ready to take the next step?

Apply now to join our next cohort and gain access to mentors, industry projects, and a supportive tech community.
