Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our Journey

KHODERS Timeline

Key milestones in our growth and impact

2017

Foundation

KHODERS Club was established at Kumasi Technical University with a mission to help students enter the tech space smoothly.

2019

First Major Project

Launched our first collaborative project, building a student management system that's still in use today.

2021

100+ Members

Reached our first major milestone of 100 active members, establishing ourselves as KTU's premier coding club.

2023

Industry Partnerships

Formed partnerships with leading tech companies to provide internship and job opportunities for our members.

2025

500+ Strong

Now with over 500 members and 80+ successful job placements, we continue to grow and impact lives.
