Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about KHODERS and our programs

How do I join KHODERS?

Joining KHODERS is simple! Fill out our contact form, attend one of our orientation sessions, or visit us at the Computer Science Department at KTU. Membership is free for all KTU students.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Not at all! KHODERS welcomes beginners and experienced programmers alike. We have programs designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced developers.

What programming languages do you teach?

We cover a wide range of technologies including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, Node.js, Flutter, and more. Our curriculum is constantly updated to match industry demands.

How much does it cost to join?

KHODERS membership is completely free for all KTU students! We believe in making quality tech education accessible to everyone.
