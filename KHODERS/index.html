Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join KHODERS Club?

Simply fill out our registration form, attend an orientation session, and you're in! We welcome students from all levels and backgrounds.

Do I need prior coding experience?

Not at all! We have programs for complete beginners as well as advanced developers. Our mentors will guide you based on your current skill level.

What programming languages do you teach?

We cover a wide range including HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Python, Java, React, Node.js, and more. Our curriculum is updated regularly to match industry demands.

Are there any membership fees?

KHODERS Club membership is completely free for all KTU students. We believe in making tech education accessible to everyone.
