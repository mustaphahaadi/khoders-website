Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining KHODERS

Is KHODERS membership really free?

Yes! KHODERS membership is completely free for all students. We believe in making quality tech education accessible to everyone, regardless of financial background.

Do I need prior programming experience?

Not at all! KHODERS welcomes complete beginners. Our programs are designed to take you from zero to hero, with personalized learning paths for every skill level.

Can non-KTU students join?

Absolutely! While KHODERS started at KTU, we welcome students from all universities and institutions across Ghana. Our community is open to all passionate learners.

What programming languages will I learn?

We cover a wide range of technologies including HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Python, Java, React, Node.js, and more. Your learning path will be customized based on your interests and career goals.

How much time do I need to commit?

We recommend at least 4-6 hours per week for optimal progress, but you can learn at your own pace. Our flexible programs accommodate different schedules and commitments.

Will I get job placement assistance?

Yes! We provide comprehensive career support including resume reviews, interview preparation, portfolio development, and connections to our industry partners for job opportunities.
